What is a Greenspace?

A piece of land with the majority of its area dedicated to vegetation, typically situated in central regions of a community.

Calculation

How We Calculate the GreenRater Score

The final GreenRater score is based on a weighted criteria. Learn More

Why are Greenspaces Important?

As harbors of low-cost recreational activity, greenspaces often serve as places of health, social, and environmental havens in commmunities.

The Problem

Greenspace maintenance can be a tedious and expensive process and therefore is often disregarded. However, when greenspaces are not adequately evaluated, their quality, and thus benefits lessen. To combat this issue, GreenRater aims to use existing geospatial knowledge & community input to develop a throrough yet efficient criteria to evaluate Fulton County’s greenspaces.
