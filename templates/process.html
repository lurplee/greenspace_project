GreenRater's Production Process

Ideation

Developing a Concept (November)

GreenRater is a continuation from a 2023 project titled ParkSpot which allowed users to view a database of local greenspaces in their area. However, instead of a simple database, GreenRater's purpose was to transform existing research into a multi-facted digital tool.

PocketPark Prototype Presentation

Method Selection

Selecting the Best Method (December)

To implement GreenRater's concept, I first needed to establish a guiding method for my research. Based on conversations with mentors and professionals in urban design space, I decided that content analysis and a digital prototype fit my project requirements the best.

Source Collection

Preparing for Content Analysis

I then started to collect my 60 sources needed for my content analysis. These greenspace-evaluation sources ranged from governmental information pamphlets to case studies using certain geospatial tools to broader academic texts about the evaluation process.
