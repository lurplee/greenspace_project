Rate Your Greenspace

Rate your local greenspace based on weighted criteria

Name of Greenspace


Accessibility (25%)

The ability to reach and use greenspaces; includes physical infrastructure barriers

Social Equity (25.2%)

The fair and inclusive distribution of the greenspace among neighborhoods and demographic groups

Connection to Nature (17.5%)

The degree of "greenness" the greenspace offers; includes grass and tree coverage, wildlife exposure and conservation

Community Consideration (16.3%)

The degree of engagement with the surrounding community; includes the frequency of community events, integration of community input

Park Features & Amenities (15.9%)

The quality and variety of the greenspace's amenities; includes bathrooms, benches, sports/exercise areas, trails and other usable public infrastructure
